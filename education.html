Education

I was always interested in the study of Medicine, since 7th grade. I even started reading medical journals at the age of 13. I am planning to get into the B.A.M.D. Program at Brooklyn College, and go to SUNY Downstate College Of Medicine. The best part of the B.A.M.D. program is that I am guaranteed a spot in Medical School, before I even start college. This program is very hard to get into, as they only accept 15 students in all of New York.
